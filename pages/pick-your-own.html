Pick Your Own

As our fruits and produce ripen, we open our gates to those that want to come out and pick themselves. We usually do pick your own (PYO) on Thursday and Friday evenings from 5pm-8pm, and Saturday/Sunday from 9am-8pm. Check out our Facebook page for PYO date announcements and our produce variety & ripening schedule for when to expect availability.
