Our Farm

Address: 7604 Beach Rd, Wadsworth, OH

Come pick your own fruit, visit our roadside stand, or shop online. You can also text or message us on Facebook anytime to arrange a pickup.

Our Offerings

What we grow: Apples, Blueberries, Strawberries, Blackberries

Garden: Plant starters (sweet potatoes, garden vegetables, strawberries, blueberries), Compost

Livestock: BSF Larvae, Live Chicks, Eggs, Laying Hens, Animal Feed

Pick Your Own

We host pick-your-own apples, blueberries, blackberries, and strawberries as they ripen. PYO typically runs Thursday and Friday evenings from 5–8 pm and weekends from 9 am–8 pm. Check our Facebook page or the ripening schedule for dates.

Roadside Stand

Our roadside stand is stocked with the things we produce here. Freezers hold frozen berries, and an order shelf lets you pick up anything you've purchased online. You'll also find our dried BSF larvae, compost, and animal feed for self-service checkout.

Web Store

Our web store offers fresh and frozen produce, honey, plant starters and other garden and livestock items for pickup. Bulk orders and local delivery are available.

We don't just grow berries—check the store for everything we offer and submit a pickup order.
